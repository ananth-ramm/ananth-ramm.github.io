What is a Margin?

Think of an HTML like a box. So you would have your content (words or an image e.g) in your website, you could add a padding around it which would create space between your content and border. You can add a border if you want your content to stick out giving it a frame. Now this is where we get to the margin, the margin is the space between your framed or non-framed content and the walls of the website. Margins allow you to make you website look nice and neat creating even spacing between your content so it doesn't look too compact and messy.
