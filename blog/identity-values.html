Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

I would say in school for a group project each individual was trying to lead the group in the direction they wanted it to go but it would lead to more disengagement and interaction between the group, so my friend and I suggested instead of trying to/make things look a certain way, we could just share ideas and key concepts between the group to first finalise our content and then brainstorm ideas for a final product and eventually came into an agreement for how the final prodcut should be and look and we received a good grade in the end. In the future I would stick to the same method, brainstorming with the group and sharing and branching off different ideas and then bringing it all together to create a satisfactory final product.
